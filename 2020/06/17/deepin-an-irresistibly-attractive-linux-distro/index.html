Developers could never get hold of beautiful things since the ages! All those terminals, black screens, and green text sometimes make me feel yuck! To be honest, UNITY and GNOME could never give us those sleek and modern transparency effects and animations.

Though Linux is, in fact, highly customizable, you have to invest a significant amount of time doing that, That's where Deepin comes to the rescue! Deepin is probably the most beautiful Linux distro out there, right from the start! Plus, you can make it your own by tons of the tweaks already available in the settings!

So, What is Deepin Exactly?

Deepin (formerly, Deepin, Linux Deepin, Hiweed GNU/Linux) is a Debian-based distribution (it was Ubuntu-based until version 15 released in late 2015) that aims to provide an elegant, user-friendly and reliable operating system. It does not only include the best the open source world has to offer, but it has also created its own desktop environment called DDE or Deepin Desktop Environment which is based on the Qt 5 toolkit. Deepin focuses much of its attention on intuitive design. Its home-grown applications, like Deepin Software Centre, DMusic and DPlayer are tailored to the average user. Being easy to install and use, deepin can be a good Windows alternative for office and home use. [Source]
