What is SweetAlert2?

As per their website, SweetAlert2 is a beautiful, responsive, customizable, accessible (WAI-ARIA) replacement for JavaScript's pop-up boxes. Zero dependencies. In other words, web devs can use SweetAlert2 to pop-up some pretty alert messages. SweetAlert2 does also support fully customized modal pop-ups for a wider range of use cases.
